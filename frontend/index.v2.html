üí° Search & Q&A Strategies


                        

                            
Search Modes

                            
    
                                
  • Keyword (0%): Strict exact word matching. Good for specific codes.
                                
    • 
                                
  • Balanced (50%): Default. Combines keywords with meaning.
    • 
                                
  • Semantic (100%): Focuses on meaning. Great for natural language
                                    queries.
    • 
                            

                        


                        

                            
Advanced Search

                            
    
                                
  • Exact phrase: "mRNA vaccine" (use quotes)
    • 
                                
  • Exclude words: cancer -mice (excludes "mice")
    • 
                                
  • OR operator: treatment OR therapy
    • 
                                
  • Author search: Use Advanced Search modal (Ctrl+Shift+F)
    • 
                            

                        


                        

                            
Filters

                            
    
                                
  • Source Type: PubMed or Clinical Trials
    • 
                                
  • Date Range: Filter by publication year
    • 
                                
  • Article Type: Research, Review, Meta-Check, etc.
    •

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions


                        

                            
What data sources are included?

                            
BioMed Scholar indexes articles from PubMed (biomedical literature) and
                                ClinicalTrials.gov (clinical trial registries).

                        


                        

                            
How does semantic search work?

                            
We use BioBERT, a language model trained on biomedical text, to understand the meaning of
                                your query. This allows finding relevant results even when exact keywords don't match.
                            

                        


                        

                            
Is my data private?

                            
Yes! Your reading list is private to your account. We don't track your individual
                                searches.

                        


                        

                            
Can I use this for medical advice?

                            
‚ö†Ô∏è BioMed Scholar is a research tool, not a substitute for professional medical advice.
                                Always consult healthcare providers for medical decisions.
