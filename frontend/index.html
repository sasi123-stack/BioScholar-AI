â“ Frequently Asked Questions


                        

                            
What data sources are included?

                            
BioMed Scholar indexes articles from PubMed (biomedical literature) and
                                ClinicalTrials.gov (clinical trial registries).

                        


                        

                            
How does semantic search work?

                            
We use BioBERT, a language model trained on biomedical text, to understand the meaning of
                                your query. This allows finding relevant results even when exact keywords don't match.

ðŸ” Search Features

                    

                        
Semantic Search

                        
Use the search bar to find biomedical articles. Our AI understands context and synonyms, so
                            searching for "heart attack" will also find articles about "myocardial infarction".

                    

                    

                        
Advanced Search

                        
Click the sliders icon in the search bar to access advanced filters including date ranges,
                            article types, and search modes.

                    

                    

                        
Search Modes

                        
Adjust the slider between Keyword (exact matches) and
                            Semantic (AI-powered understanding) to control search behavior.
